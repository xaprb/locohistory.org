The 1874 Woman's Festival in Charlottesville, VA

Local historian Gayle Schulman discovered a letter published in 1874 about a remarkable woman’s rights event. It was written by Philena Carkin a noted educator of freedmen in Charlottesville, Virginia. The pdf file available below contains Ms. Schulman’s introduction to the historic letter as well as her 2011 transcript of it from the Woman's Journal.

To download a copyrighted version of this annotated letter (as a pdf file), click here.

Topics covered in the article:
Women's suffrage; women's rights; reconstuction education; Jefferson school; Charottesville.

People discussed in the article:
Philena Carkin, Anna Gardner, Mrs. E.D. Cheney, Harriet B. Stowe, Mary A. Livermore, Rosa Hull, Maria Lewis, Augusta Lewis, Harriet Wayne, William Jones, Julia Ward Howe, Henry Browne Blackwell, Lucy Stone.
